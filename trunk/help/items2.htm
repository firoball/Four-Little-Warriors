Special Items

About items

The items listed here do not trigger events. This is typical for items which only affect a single player or which have a ranged effect like an explosion.

Power Crystal

Collect as many power crystals as possible since they are the key to win the game. But never forget that golems like crystals too - and never udnerestimate your opponents.

Affects: One player

Mine

Be careful! This mean item makes you lose your crystals once it explodes nearby. If this wasn't wnough, the explosion knocks down any player in range.

Affects: All players nearby

Shield

After collecting this item, the player gains invulnerability for a short time period. Now it's your chance to attack other players and kill some golems.

Affects: One player

Cape

You gain limited invisibility for a few seconds. The enemy players can't spot you that easy anymore, but take care not to lose your own warrior, since you'll see him worse as well.

Affects: One player

Magnet

The crystal magnet drags all nearby crystals to you for a limited time period. Use this item to collect as many crystals as you can.

Affects: One player and nearby crystals

Fireball

This special weapon features spreadshot fireballs. Make good use of this advantage.

Affects: One player

Acidball

When collecting this weapon you will be able to shoot bouncing acidballs. With each collision the acid ball gains speed, which makes it difficult to escape.

Affects: One player

Lightning

A special weapon which shows random behaviour.The lightning which is emitted when attacking can change direction and might also split up into several other lightnings.

Affects: One player
