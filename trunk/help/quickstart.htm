Quickstart

Select Player

From Main Menu first choose Start Game and hit the Enter key.
Next you'll find yourself on the player selection screen. By hitting the Fire button (CTRL by default for yellow player) you can activate your player. Use the Movement keys (cursor keys by default for yellow player) to modify the look of the CPU players and your own player. You cannot modify other human players.
In case you're using a gamepad or joystickyou have the possibility to switch between two control modes:
- up/down to move forward/backward and left/right to turn
- move directly into the pressed direction

When done, press Fire button again. A small check will show up on the lower right of your player panel.
Only after all human players are ready (all checks must be visible), you can continue by pressing the Enter key.

Select Level

Use Cursor keys to select the desired level. Once you are finished, hit Enter key to proceed. By hitting the ESC key you can return to the Player Selection menu at any time.

That's all you need to know for now... for fine tuning and setup of input devices, see Game Options screen.

Good fight!
