Event Items

About events

The items listed here trigger events. An event shows up on screen with a short animated message to prepare to what is coming up next. Events are mainly triggered by items, but can also be started by switches and traps.
All players are affected by events.

Speed Up

Increases game speed. Can lead to confusion. Players should pay more attention at what is happening around them while this powerup is active.

Affects: All players and golems

Slowmotion

Reduce movement speed. This item influences all players and golems and it gives the player more precise control over his character. Bullet speed and timers are not affected by this special item.

Affects: All players and golems

Change Positions

Players randomly change positions. This can bring some confusion into gameplay, so be careful.

Affects: All players

Rotation

The camera suddenly rotates and shows the battle arena from another direction. Like the Change Positions special item, this one can easily cause confusion.

Affects: Camera

Monopoly

The player who collected this item will gain several crystals from his opponents. This item can be used to suddenly change leadership.

Affects: All Players

Equalize

All crystals are taken and shared between all players in fair amounts. This item can be used to catch up with leading players when your crystal count is low - but it can also ruin your leadership.

Affects: All Players

Upside Down

The camera is turned upside down for a limtied time period. Be careful not to get lost!

Affects: Camera

Super Power

A strong crystal energy is released. Every crystal inside the arena grows and counts twice when collected. Try to get some crystals before they turn back to normal.

Affects: All crystals
