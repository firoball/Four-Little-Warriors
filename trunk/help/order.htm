How to register your version

Free restricted Version

By downloading Four Little Warriors you already obtained the free version of the game. This version is restricted to two playable levels and two selectable players.

By skipping the registration process the restricted free version of the game is started. You may play the free demo as often as you want and as long as you want. There are no other restrictions.

Experience how much fun this game is - if you like it, you can unlock your installation to the unrestricted version at any time!

Unrestricted Version

The unrestricted version of Four Little Warriors offers three more fun levels and two more characters ready for battle. Experience all three special weapons when fighting your opponents.
You don't even need to download the game again - your restricted version can be unlocked to the unrestricted version within a few minutes! Once you entered your personal registration details, the registry screen will disappear together with all demo messages.

Please remember: Your license is personalized and must not be given to other persons!
